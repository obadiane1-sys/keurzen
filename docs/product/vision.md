# Product Vision

## What is Keurzen?
Keurzen is a premium household coordination app designed to reduce invisible labor, rebalance responsibilities, and make the daily load easier to understand and share.

## Core promise
A calmer household starts with clearer visibility.

## Main value propositions
- shared tasks become visible
- time spent becomes measurable
- mental load becomes discussable
- imbalance becomes actionable
- budget effort becomes more transparent

## Target users
- couples
- families
- colocations
- people who need structure without guilt

## Tone
- warm
- elegant
- non-judgmental
- reassuring
- precise

## What Keurzen is not
- not a gamified chore app
- not a punitive productivity tracker
- not a chaotic all-in-one platform
- not a financial accounting tool

## UX principles
- one thing at a time
- premium simplicity
- soft but clear hierarchy
- useful insights over noisy dashboards
- make invisible load visible without blame
